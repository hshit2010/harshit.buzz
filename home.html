FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process.

What services do you offer?

I offer social media audits, graphic design, content writing, and comprehensive social media management services. Each service can be customized to fit your specific needs and budget.

How can I contact you?

You can reach me through the contact form on this website, via email at contact@harshit.buzz, or through WhatsApp. I typically respond within 24 hours.

What are your payment options?

I accept various payment methods including bank transfers, UPI, and digital wallets. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion for larger projects.

What is your revision policy?

I include up to 2 rounds of revisions for all design and content projects to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final deliverables.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Single graphics can be delivered in 1-2 days, while comprehensive social media strategies may take 1-2 weeks to develop.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes! I understand that every business has unique needs. I offer customized packages that combine various services at discounted rates for long-term collaborations.
